SHOPPING BAG ETHNOGRAPHY

Visualizing the Material Culture of Consumption

01. THE DRIFTING PATH

Spatiotemporal trajectory connected by the cost of time and money.

  • Path Color: Weather (Warm=Sunny, Cool=Rain/Cloudy)
  • Line Thickness: Time Cost (Thicker = Harder to get)
  • Node Halo: Monetary Cost (Larger Glow = More Expensive)
  • Inner Dot: Access Route (Solid=Offline, Hollow=Online)

02. THE LIVING ARCHIVE

A memory palace where the physical state of the bag dictates its virtual form.

  • Height: Colorfulness
  • Transparency: Newness (Faded = Old/Worn)
  • Animation: Monetary Cost (Floating = Expensive)
  • Surface: Cleanliness (Matte = Dirty, Shiny = Clean)
  • Wireframe: Online Route

03. THE BRAND GRAVITY

Brand solar systems defined by motivation and visual impact.

  • Orbit: Brands (Centers of Gravity)
  • Shape: Motivation (Circle=Memorial, Diamond=Interest, etc.)
  • Link Thickness: Pattern Proportion (Visual Loudness)
  • Link Style: Solid=Clean, Dashed=Worn
